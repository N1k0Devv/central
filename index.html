A Quiet Garden & Terrace
Stay in Kutaisi

Green Rooms is a 3-star stay in Kutaisi known for its quiet location, garden, and terrace. It offers a calmer base within a 10-20 minute walk of the city center.

Landmarks are easy to reach on foot: White Bridge is about 16 minutes away, Kutaisi State Historical Museum is around 1.8 km, and Bagrati Cathedral is about a 24-minute walk.
