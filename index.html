About Us

A Legacy of Georgian
Hospitality

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Kutaisi, Central Hotel stands as a beacon of luxury and comfort. Our hotel seamlessly blends traditional Georgian warmth with contemporary elegance, offering guests an unforgettable experience in one of the Caucasus region's most captivating cities.

From our meticulously designed rooms to our world-class dining, every detail has been crafted to ensure your stay is nothing short of extraordinary. Whether you're exploring the ancient Bagrati Cathedral, wandering through the Prometheus Cave, or simply relaxing in our spa, Central Hotel is your perfect home away from home.
